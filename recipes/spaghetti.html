Spaghetti Agalio e Olio

Description

This recipe offers simplicity to cook with wonderful flavor. It relies on natural flavors, and using high quality olive is important. Not many ingredients are necessary for this masterpiece. It features spaghetti tossed in olive oil that's been infused with sauteed garlic. It's a perfect meal for quick and simple preparation without sacraficing flavor or quality.

List of Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225grams) spaghetti noodles
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • Salt
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salt water. Add the noodles in and cook until al dente or softer, your preference. Before draining the pasta, scoop out about 1/2 a cup of the salt water you boiled the noodles with, we may use it later.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the thinkly sliced garlic. Sautee the garlic until it turns golden brown. Be careful to not burn it.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat it in the garlic-infused olive oil. If it appears dry, you can add some of the water you saved to help create a light sauce.
  4. Season the pasta with your preferred amount of salt.
  5. Add the chopped parsley and parmesan cheese to the top of the dish, and serve!
