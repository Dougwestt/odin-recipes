Chicken Pot Pie

Description

This makes a perfect and hearty entree for either lunch or dinner with not much hassle to prepare! We have compiled a recipe for a simple yet delicious dish, but feel free to add in other ingredients that you may desire!

List of Ingredients

  1. 2 cups of cooked chicken, diced (rotisserie chicken works well)
  2. 1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
  3. 1 can of mixed vegetables (peas carrots and corn)
  4. 1 sheet of refridgerated pie crust
  5. Salt and pepper

Instuctions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Combined the diced chicken, condensed cream of chicken soup, salt/pepper, and vegtables together and stir well.
  3. Transfer mixture to a baking dish
  4. Place pie crust over the top of the baking dish, covering the chicken and vegtable mixture. Press down the edges of the crust to seal it with the dish.
  5. Cut a few slits in the crust allowing it to steam during baking.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Let cool before serving and enjoy!
